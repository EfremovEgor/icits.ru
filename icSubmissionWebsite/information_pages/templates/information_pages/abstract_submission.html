Abstract Submission

Abstracts of original presentations are solicited for inclusion in the SCO/CIS programme. To facilitate the submission process and subsequent follow-up process, please consider the following guidelines, information and deadlines:

  • Abstracts should be submitted by 15th april 2024 via an online submission tool.
  • Authors select their preferred symposium to which they submit abstracts.
  • The authors indicate the preferred type of report (oral or remote).
  • Authors indicate their full name, organization and email address, including for all co-authors.
  • Abstracts must be written in plain text and must not contain tables, figures or photographs.
  • The title of abstracts should be limited to 30 words.
  • Abstract text should be limited to 500 words (<1 A4 page)
  • Submission of abstracts confirms your consent to publication of abstracts in official conference materials.

Instruction of paper/presentation submission

Upon completion of abstract review, accepted abstracts will be included in the conference program and a corresponding notification will be sent to the authors. For selected abstracts, full articles and original presentations must be uploaded. Please take into account the following recommendations, information and deadlines:

  • Full articles and presentations must be submitted by May 19, 2024 via the online application.
  • Articles must be prepared in the format specified in the template here and uploaded in PDF format.
  • The full article must be a minimum of two pages and a maximum of eight pages.
  • Presentation slides must be prepared in 16:9 format and uploaded in .pptx format.
  • Submitting your article and presentation confirms your consent to publication in the official conference materials.
