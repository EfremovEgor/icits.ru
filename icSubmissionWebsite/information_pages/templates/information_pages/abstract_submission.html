Abstract Submission

Abstracts of original presentations are solicited for inclusion in the ICITS 2024 programme. To facilitate the submission process and subsequent follow-up process, please consider the following guidelines, information and deadlines:

  • Abstracts should be submitted by 14th February 2024 via an online submission tool.
  • Principle author or contact person to provide full name and contact information.
  • Please also include a short, one paragraph, biography of the presenter in the body of the email.
  • Author(s) to indicate their preferred presentation type (oral or poster).
  • Abstracts are to be written in plain text and should not include tables, figures, or photographs.
  • Submission of an abstract acknowledges your acceptance for the abstract to be published in the official conference proceedings.
  • Abstract title - must be limited to 50 words.
  • Abstract text - limited to approximately 700 words (<1 page of A4)
