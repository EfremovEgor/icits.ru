To facilitate the submission process and subsequent follow-up process, please consider the following guidelines, information and deadlines:

  • Abstracts should be submitted by April 15, 2024 via online submission tool.
  • Authors select the preferred symposium and topic to which they submit abstracts.
  • Authors indicate the preferred type of report (on-site or online).
  • Authors indicate their full name, organization and email address, including for all co-authors.
  • Abstracts must be written in plain text and must not contain tables, figures or photographs.
  • The title of abstracts should be limited to 30 words.
  • Abstract text should be limited to 500 words.
  • Submission of abstracts confirms your consent to publication of abstracts in official conference materials.
