About

The ICITS is a series of conferences providing an international forum for specialists in the field of space flight dynamics. It is sponsored by RUDN Univeristy

Areas of interest for the ICITS include, but are not limited to:

  • Intelligent and technical systems
  • Mechanical engineering and instrument making
  • Architecture and construction
  • Geology, mining and oil and gas engineering
  • Management and economics in industry
