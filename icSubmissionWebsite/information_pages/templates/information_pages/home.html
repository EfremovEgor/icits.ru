About

SCO/CIS Conference on Information and Technical Systems is taking place on May 28-31, 2024 in Moscow, Russia.

The conference is jointly organized by RUDN University and universities of Shanghai Cooperation Organization and Commonwealth of Independent States.

The Hybrid format is supposed. We encourage researchers to take part in face-to-face activities, but as for the willing of the participants, it is possible an online experience as well.

Papers for the conferences shall be selected basing on the quality of 500 word abstracts. For accepted papers presentations and complete manuscripts are to be submitted. Conference proceedings will be published by RUDN University. The best papers will be recommended to publish in leading Russian journals (K1/K2 categories).

Working languages: Russian and English.

Conference includes the following symposiums:

  • Intelligent Technical Systems
  • Mechanical Engineering and Instrument Making
  • Architecture and Construction
  • Geology, Mining and Oil-and-gas Engineering
  • Management and Economics in Industry
