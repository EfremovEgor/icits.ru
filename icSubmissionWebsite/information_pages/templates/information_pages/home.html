The SCO/CIS Conference on Information and Technical Systems is taking place on May 28-31, 2024 in Russia, Moscow

The conference is jointly organized by RUDN University and universities of countries included in the Shanghai Cooperation Organization and Commonwealth of Independent States.

Conference languages are Russian and English.

Conference includes the following symposiums:

  • Intelligent Technical Systems
  • Mechanical Engineering and Instrument Making
  • Architecture and Construction
  • Geology, Mining and Oil-and-gas Engineering
  • Management and Economics in Industry

Symposium “Intelligent Technical Systems”

Chair:

Yury N. Razoumny

Director of Academy of Engineering of RUDN University
Dr.Sc. (tech), Professor

Topics:

  • artificial intelligence and robotics;
  • information security;
  • intelligent space life cycle management
  • machine learning and big geodata.
